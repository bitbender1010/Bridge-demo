Frequently asked questions
We are always ready to answer your questions, contact us if you have more questions about how we operate.
Q. What does physical and remote tasks mean?
A. Physical tasks means you want the person doing the task to be there physically (example: Electrician or Plumber).

Remote tasks means the person doing the task doesn't need to be physically present and can work remotely (example: Logo designer or Digital marketer)
Q. Is Bridge safe to use?
A. Yes, Bridge is 100% safe to use. We run background checks on our users, most especially for people who will be engaging in physical tasks like cleaning and laundry.

So you are rest assured that nothing will go wrong when you hire someone on Bridge.
Q. How does Bridge ensure my client pays me after a completed job?
A. Before any worker starts any job on Bridge, we receive a full payment from the client at first and may only release a particular percentage on request during the duration of the job.

When a task has been duly completed and confirmed, we release the remaining payment to the worker. This is to ensure that no worker is being owed while transacting on Bridge
Q. Is Bridge just another freelancing platform?
A. Bridge is more than a freelancing plaform, one of he things we promote is discouraging the notion that freelancing has to be a rat race for price.

That is why we allow our users to set their budget range and we also disabled user comments under very task, this is to ensure one seller's price is not influenced by what other sellers or service providers are offering.
