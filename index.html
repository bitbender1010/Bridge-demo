Frequently asked questions
We are always ready to answer your questions, contact us if you have more questions about how we operate.
Q. What do you mean by "Local Neighborhood Jobs"
A. "Local Neighborhood Jobs" are basically in-formal jobs (they include but are not limited to); electrical and plumbing, appliance repair and installation, cleaning and laundry, tutoring, driving etc.
Q. Is Bridge safe to use?
A. Yes, Bridge is 100% safe to use, we run a series of background checks and verification on our users.

How ever you are advised to use your decression when you don't feel safe about an hire, report to us and we will take care of it.
Q. How does Bridge ensure my client pays me after a completed job?
A. Before any worker commences any job on Bridge, we receive a full payment from the client at first and may only release a particular percentage on request during the duration of the job.

When a task has been duly completed and confirmed, we release the remaining payment to the worker. This is to ensure that no worker is being owed on our platform and to also assure the client gets a satisfactory result.
Q. How can I start using Bridge?
A. If you are an employer click the "Post a job" button, complete the form requesting for your job details, and click "Submit" to post your job. You will get a recommendation email from us in 24 hours, recommending to you the best hand for your task.

And if you are a service provider and want to register and start making money on Bridge, click the "Render a service" button. Complete the form and submit it, you will get a confirmation email from us in 24 hours, after then you can start receiving job offers on Bridge.
