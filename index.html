Frequently asked questions
We are always ready to answer your questions, contact us if you have more questions about how we operate.
Q. What does physical and remote tasks mean?
A. Physical tasks or work means you want the person doing the task to be there physically (example: Electrician or Plumber).

Remote tasks or work means the person doing the task doesn't need to be physically present and can work from anywhere of the world. (example: Logo designer or Digital marketer)
Q. Is Bridge safe to use?
A. Yes, Bridge is 100% safe to use. We run background checks on our users, most especially for people who will be engaging in physical tasks like Cleaning, Grocery Shopping etc.

So you are rest assured that nothing will go wrong when you hire someone on Bridge.
Q. How does Bridge ensure my client pays me after a completed job?
A. Before any worker starts any job on Bridge, we receive a full payment from the client at first and may only release a particular percentage on request during the duration of the job.

When a task has been duly completed and confirmed, we release the remaining payment to the worker. This is to ensure that no worker is being owed on Bridge and to also assure the client gets a satisfactory result.
Q. How can I start using Bridge?
A. After you click the "Post a task" button, fill the form requesting for your task details, when you are done filling the form click "Submit" to post your task. You will get a recommendation email from us in 24 hours, recommending you to the best professional for your job.

And if you are a service provider and want to register and start making money on Bridge, click the "Render a service" button. Complete the form and submit it, you will get a confirmation email from us in 24 hours, after then you can start receiving job offers on Bridge.
